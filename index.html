Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

PolishAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Key Point: We process text only to provide the service. We do not store, sell, or share your text content with third parties.

Information We Collect

Text Content: When you use PolishAI, we temporarily process the text you select to provide AI-powered rewriting. This text is sent to our secure servers, processed by AI, and immediately discarded after the response is returned.

Usage Data: We track anonymous usage counts (number of polishes per day) stored locally on your device to enforce free tier limits.

Purchase Information: If you purchase Pro, we store your email address to verify your license. Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe - we never see or store your payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the text polishing service
  • To verify Pro license status
  • To improve our service
  • To communicate about your purchase (if applicable)

Data Storage & Security

Your selected text is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission.

License data is stored securely in our database with encryption at rest. We use Supabase for secure data storage with SOC 2 Type II compliance.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For AI text processing. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: For payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: For license data storage. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy
  • Vercel: For API hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy

Your Rights

  • Request information about data we hold
  • Opt out of any marketing communications
